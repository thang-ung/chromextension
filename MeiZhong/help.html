Mouse buttons
  • wheel forward (up) to move to next range / pad
  • wheel forward (up) while hovering a glyph of a "radical" pad to load pad with associated glyphs
  • wheel backward (down) to move back to previous pad
  • wheel backward with extra force to move back to first pad
  • wheel backward (down) +Shift-key to move to baseline --first pad of radicals
